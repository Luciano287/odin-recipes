Description

A Recipe formed from multiple near-empty boxes of Pasta, this dish is simple and perfect for a cold day, involving peppers and cayenne for a small spicy touch.

Ingredients

  • 8oz Dry Pastas of Choice, preferably Penne, Rigatoni, or similarly shaped and size
  • 200g Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Green and Red Pepper each, 1/2 Onion
  • 3-6 Cloves Garlic
  • Smoked Paprika, Cayenne, Cumin, Oregano, Black Pepper
