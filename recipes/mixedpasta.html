Description

A Recipe formed from multiple near-empty boxes of Pasta, this dish is simple and perfect for a cold day, involving peppers and cayenne for a small spicy touch.

Ingredients

  • 8oz Dry Pastas of Choice, preferably Penne, Rigatoni, or similarly shaped and size
  • 200g Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp White Vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 Green and Red Pepper each, 1/2 Onion
  • 3-6 Cloves Garlic
  • Smoked Paprika, Cayenne, Cumin, Oregano, Black Pepper

Preparation

  1. Boil 8oz Pasta for approximately 7m or until Al Dente.
  2. Drain ~75% of Pasta water, add Tomato Sauce, Garlic, 1 Tbsp Olive Oil, Peppers, and Seasonings.
  3. Simmer for 15-30m or until most of the liquid is gone.
  4. Dress with second Tbsp of Olive Oil and another sprinkle of black pepper.
