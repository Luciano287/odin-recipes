Description

Tofu is a blank slate, and one of the best ways to enjoy it is by oven roasting it with various spices, especially spices to give it a rich flavour and slight spicy sting. Smoked Paprika, Black Pepper, Cayenne, and Garlic define the seasoned flavours of this dish.

Ingredients

  • One Block of Extra Firm Tofu
  • 1-2 Tbsp of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Smoked Paprika, Cayenne, Black Pepper, Garlic Powder, Salt and Parsely
