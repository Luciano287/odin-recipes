Description

Aglio E Olio is a simple, convenient yet comforting and delicious pasta dish involving a plentiful bout of olive oil toasted garlic and chili flakes, into which Spaghetti is tossed and topped with parsely, black pepper, and the juice of a lemon.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of Dry Spaghetti of Choice
  • 1/2 cup of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Six Cloves of thinly slices Garlic
  • Red Pepper flakes, Black Pepper, Parsely
  • The Juice of 1 Lemon

Steps

  1. Boil the Spaghetti until Al Dente
  2. In a Pan, toast Garlic and Red Pepper Flakes
  3. Toss in Spaghetti, turn off the heat, then top with Black Pepper, Parsely, and the juice of a lemon.
